Whereabout

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 18, 2026

Short version: Whereabout stores all your location data privately on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Overview

Whereabout is a personal location-tracking app that records where you have been throughout the day. This privacy policy describes what data the app collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

Data Collected

Whereabout collects the following data while location tracking is enabled:

  • Location records — GPS coordinates, timestamp, altitude, speed, and accuracy.
  • Visit records — Places where you stayed, with arrival and departure times, and a reverse-geocoded address derived on-device using Apple's CoreLocation framework.

No other data is collected. The app does not access your contacts, calendar, photos, microphone, or camera.

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Data never leaves your device — there are no servers, no cloud sync, and no backups managed by this app.

If you use iCloud Backup, your device's local data (including app data) may be included in your personal iCloud Backup, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Whereabout does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no crash-reporting services embedded in the app.

Location Permissions

Whereabout requests access to your location. For background tracking to work, the app requests "Always" location permission. You can change or revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. Revoking permission stops new data from being recorded; existing data on your device is not affected.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained on your device until you delete the app. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children's Privacy

Whereabout is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.
